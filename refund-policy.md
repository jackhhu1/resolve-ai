# Refund Policy — Support Team

## Standard refunds
Any support agent may approve a refund up to $150 without manager approval,
provided the following conditions are met:
- The order exists in the system
- The refund amount matches the original order amount
- The order was placed within the last 90 days
- The reason is one of: wrong item, damaged goods, delayed shipment

## Elevated refunds ($150–$500)
Requires manager approval. A Jira escalation ticket must be created.
The ticket must include: order ID, customer name, amount, and reason.

## Refunds above $500
Require finance team sign-off. Escalate via Jira with priority: High.
